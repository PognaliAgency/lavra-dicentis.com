ABOUT THE DESIGN

A kitchen sponge holder reimagined through the lens of classical heritage and postmodern critique. Inspired by the expressive masks of ancient Greek theatre, this product merges artistic symbolism with functional design. The mask, once used to convey complex emotions on stage, now serves as a sculptural yet practical household object.
